About Me

My interest in technology began early, with a strong fascination for robotics and programming throughout middle school and high school. Between 2014 and 2020, I actively participated in robotics competitions through my schools, which helped establish a solid foundation in problem-solving, teamwork, and technical thinking.

During my studies in Computer Technology, I developed skills in programming, web design, and database concepts, including hands-on experience with HTML and CSS. One of my first major projects involved designing and developing a full-stack website, where I gained practical experience with both front-end and back-end development and strengthened my knowledge of C++.

This experience culminated in my associate degree capstone project, where I collaborated with a team to design a fully functional hospital model. The project included developing a website, creating a floor plan, managing a budget, and addressing security considerations. Through this work, I gained valuable insight into the business and operational aspects of technology, as well as a deeper appreciation for its real-world applications. Building on this foundation, I later applied my skills at the university level by working as part of a team to develop a scholarship management tool for a client. This project enhanced my ability to collaborate effectively under tight deadlines and high-pressure conditions. In addition to development contributions, I authored tutorials and user manuals for administrators and end users, ensuring the system was accessible, well-documented, and easy to maintain.
